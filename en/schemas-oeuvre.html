Diagrams of the Work

Seeing the major movements: not to simplify the work, but to enter more quickly into its deep articulations.

The diagrams below are pedagogical maps made for this site. They do not replace the books, and they do not claim to give an official order. Their function is to make visible a recurring dynamic in Almaas: an ordinary experience is looked at precisely, the defensive structure shows itself, and then a quality of Being may appear where one thought there was only lack.

1. Theory of holes

Quality
Love, strength, value
An essential presence available at first.
Loss
Cut off from awareness
The quality is no longer recognized or supported.
Hole
Felt lack
Void, shame, powerlessness, need.
Filling
False aspect
Projection, belief, image or behavior.
Inquiry
Staying with it
Feeling without fleeing or filling.
Return
Quality recognized
Presence returns from within.

The reversal is essential: one does not look for a better filling; one crosses the hole toward the quality it signals.

2. Inquiry

Start
Present experience
Sensation, emotion, thought, defense.
Attitude
Openness
No fixed position, no quick conclusion.
Door
Not-knowing
Letting experience reveal what it is.
Engine
Living question
Curiosity, love of truth, personal thread.
Guidance
Discrimination
Diamond Guidance refines true and false.
Fruit
Understanding
A more objective perception integrates.

Inquiry is not a technique for obtaining a state. It is conscious participation in the unfolding of experience.

3. Inner judge

Attack
You should
Criticism, shame, moral pressure.
Effect
Contraction
The body closes, experience hides.
Defense
Obey or rebel
Two ways of remaining caught by the judge.
Understand
See the function
Old protection, internalized standards.
Support
Strength and compassion
No longer fleeing, no longer brutalizing oneself.
Opening
Simple truth
Experience can show itself as it is.

4. Pearl and Point

The Pearl

The Personal Essence: not a facade personality, but an integrated personal presence, capable of relationship, action and autonomy.

The Point

The Essential Identity: a simple center of presence, deeper than self-image and the narcissistic need to be confirmed.

In this region of the work, Almaas does not deny the person: he distinguishes the person constructed around representations from the person becoming transparent to Being. The need to be seen, support, narcissistic hurt and the empty shell become passages toward an identity that is less imaginary and more real.

6. Enneagram and essence

Holy view
Objective reality
A true way of seeing Being.

Here again, the point is not to label a person, but to recover the objective view that fixation replaces.

How to use these diagrams

For a first reading

Read each line as a story of transformation: an ordinary experience, a defense, a quality, then a possible integration.

For deeper reading

Choose one diagram, then open the associated page and look in your own experience for where that dynamic repeats.

Diagrams are useful because they show structure. Their limitation is that they can give the illusion of understanding too quickly. In Almaas, real understanding begins when a diagram becomes felt: the hole is sensed, the judge is recognized in an inner sentence, inquiry becomes a living question, the Pearl or the Point stop being words and become a direction of experience.

Moving from diagram to perception

For these maps to truly serve, they need to be read as lenses of perception. A diagram is not only a sequence of boxes: it proposes a way to look at an inner scene. The question becomes: where am I in the movement? Am I at the level of the symptom, the defense, the self-image, the hole, or already in contact with an essential quality?

Ordinary scene

I feel ignored. The holes diagram asks: which lack has been activated? Love, value, support, recognition? The judge diagram asks: which inner sentence is attacking this lack?

Turning point

Instead of immediately seeking reassurance, inquiry stays with the experience. The lack becomes more precise, then may reveal the quality whose imprint it carried.

Psychological reading

The diagrams show defenses, object relations, self-images, ideals, and fixations organizing experience.

Spiritual reading

The same diagrams show how a quality of Being, a sacred view, or a wider dimension can become accessible again within experience.

Working sources: Diamond Heart, Essence, The Pearl Beyond Price, The Point of Existence, Spacecruiser Inquiry, the love guides and the enneagram books. The formulations are original syntheses.
