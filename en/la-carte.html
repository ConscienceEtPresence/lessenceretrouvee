The map

The whole work in eight movements — how the notions hold together.

Almaas's work spans dozens of volumes and hundreds of notions — yet it holds together in a single overall gesture. Here is that gesture, in eight movements. Every underlined term opens the glossary at the right entry: this map is made for wandering.

True Naturemanifests asBeing, presence, consciousness, love, intelligence, emptiness
Essencedifferentiates intothe essential aspects — Strength, Love, Joy, Peace, Will, Compassion, Brilliancy…
The soulcan integrateEssence, personality, body and relationships
The loss of an aspectis experienced asa hole, a deficiency
The egomaintains its identity throughimages, object relations, defenses and compensations
Inquiryis guided bycuriosity, the love of truth and the Diamond Guidance
Realizationbecomes human throughactualization, embodiment, integration and maturation
The boundless dimensionsrevealthe nonduality of presence and forms

The site walks this unfolding door by door: the path follows its thread from the nearest to the vastest. For a more direct entrance, begin with the 12 key notions.
