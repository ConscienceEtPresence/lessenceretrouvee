Essence Regained

after A. H. Almaas

There is something in you that has never disappeared. Life has covered it over — it has not destroyed it. This site explores the question of essence, guided by the work of A. H. Almaas — contemporary mystical thinker and spiritual teacher, founder of the Diamond Approach.
