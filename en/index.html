Essence Regained

after A. H. Almaas

There is something in you that has never disappeared. Life has covered it over — it has not destroyed it. This site explores the question of essence, guided by the work of A. H. Almaas — contemporary mystical thinker and spiritual teacher, founder of the Diamond Approach.

Is something missing?

An emptiness in the chest, a sense of not truly being yourself, a strength that's missing, a love sought outside yourself — for Almaas, a precise lack can become a door : behind some “holes”, the very quality that seemed absent may reveal itself. This part of the site will start from what you're living — not from theory.
